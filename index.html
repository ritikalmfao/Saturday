Pizza

A fluffy base with a pool of decadent melted cheese over a layer of smoky tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

Base-

  • 875g All purpose flour
  • 700mL Water
  • 2tsp Salt
  • 13g Active dry yeast
  • 3tsp Sugar
  • 3tbsp Olive oil

Tomato sauce-

  • 1 Large onion, chopped finely
  • 4 Large tomatoes, chopped
  • 2tsp ground black pepper
  • 3tsp Salt
  • 1/2tbsp Lemon juice
  • 100mL Water
  • 1tbsp Olive oil
  • 1tbsp minced garlic
  • 2tsp Sugar

Cheese-

  • 100g Shredded cheddar
  • 150g Shredded mozzarella
  • 100g Grated Parmesan
  • 50g Smoked gouda

Procedure:

Base-

  • Step 1- Pour 700mL of lukewarm water into a large bowl
  • Step 2- Add the sugar and yeast into the water and stir
  • Step 3- Set aside for 10 minutes to activate the yeast
  • Step 4- Into a separate bowl, add the flour and whisk the salt into it
  • Step 5- After the yeast activates, pour the mixture into the dry ingredients along with the olive oil
  • Step 6- Knead thoroughly for 10 minutes till the dough springs back on poking with a finger
  • Step 7- Shape the dough into a ball and place into a bowl greased with oil
  • Step 8- Cover with saran wrap or a towel and set aside to rise for 2 hours
  • Step 9- After the 2 hours, divide the dough into 4 equal parts, knead, and set aside to rise for another hour

Tomato Sauce-

  • Step 1- Into a saucepan, add the olive oil and crank up the heat to medium
  • Step 2- Once the oil gets hot, add in the garlic, onion, and salt
  • Step 3- Once the onion caramelizes, add the chopped tomatoes and water. Mix and simmer for 2 minutes
  • Step 4- Now, add in the lemon juice, pepper, and sugar
  • Step 5- Simmer for another 3 minutes and set aside

Final preparation-

  • Step 1- Roll out a piece of dough and lightly poke with a fork all over the surface of the dough
  • Step 2- Scoop about 1/4cup of tomato sauce and spread it onto the dough
  • Step 3- Mix up the 4 shredded cheeses
  • Step 4- Add as much cheese as you like to the pizza and place on your favourite toppings
  • Step 5- Transfer the pizza onto a pizza stone or greased baking tray
  • Step 6- Bake at 220°C for 12 minutes

Enjoy~
